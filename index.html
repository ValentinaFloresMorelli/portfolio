Hello! And welcome to my portfolio!

It was created with great care and dedication, and it is constantly updated as I complete new projects.

A little about me: my name is Valentina. Of my 24 years of life, I spent 7 working in the tourism restoration sector. It was an incredible experience that not only gave me a career, but also took me to some of the most breathtaking places on Earth—and even gave me a great love story. However, that chapter came to an end, and it was time for change. I decided to ground myself and take a brave step: going back to study. This time, I chose something I had never imagined doing, but that had always piqued my curiosity—something that matches who I truly am.

After a lot of research, the Professional Data Analytics Certificate seemed like the right and most complete way to start this new journey. Through it, I gained solid knowledge in areas like data cleaning, data visualization, and tools such as SQL, R programming, Tableau, and Power BI. Along with my diploma, a capstone project, and several other projects that you’ll find here, I am now eager and fully prepared to secure my first opportunity as a data analyst.

I speak multiple languages, and you can find more details about my skills and experiences in my CV attached here. Feel free to explore my work — I would be thrilled to hear from you! It was a pleasure to share a little bit of my story. ✨
